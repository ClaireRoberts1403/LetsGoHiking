About us


"Adventure is worthwhile in itself" - Amelia Earhart

Let's go hiking provides you with inspiration to get into the great outdoors join a online social community who like to hike and who love a challenge.


  • Click on the link to Challenges and you will find hiking challenges that you can have an adventure whilst completing and get a great award.

  • When you've found a challenge you want to do, add it to your basket and once purchased you'll get access to the full route which will guide you step by step. Your award will also be shipped and recieved within 5 working days.

  • These awards will be patches or stickers individually designed and exclusive to purchasing the challenge.

  • As a member you will then be able to share your adventure with other members through the community blog. This allows your hike to become more of an event and help you to create long lasting memories.

  • Also check out the 'Let's go hiking' shop, for more patches, stickers and more.
